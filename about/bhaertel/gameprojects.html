Kill-A-Watt

Kill A Watt is a fast pace multiplayer networked game that pits two players against each other as they try to out strategize and out capture their opponent. Rather than a tower defense, its a tower attack. Two players take control of electricity minions to fight over power generators and shock tower control. Kill A Watt is a game of numbers, movement, and strategy. Two players battle over strategic points in hopes of eliminating the opposing force. Players can achieve victory by taking all of their opponents towers.
